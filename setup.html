On-The-Go

For this, I went with the 4th Gen iPad Pro 12.9

  • Storage:

    256GB

  • RAM:

    6GB

  • Case/Keyboard:

    Brydge Keyboard

  • Accessories:

    Apple Pencil 2nd Gen

I went with this iPad because of it's capabilities and it's screen size. The fact that it boasts a 12.9 inch display and 6GB of RAM, makes it a portable powerhouse for coding and editing. The Brydge keyboard was the clear winner for me, once you attach the iPad to it, it essentially turns the iPad Pro into a MacBook Air with a touch screen. The upside to having an iPad of this size of course the Sidecar feature, using the iPad's 12.9 inch display as a secondary one for the MacBook Pro, helps make the iPad Pro a must have for any on-the-go multitasker. The Apple Pencil really hasn't found it's way into daily use for me just yet, but it's an awesome accessory to have.


Audio

For audio, I use a pair of Apple Airpods Max.


I went with Airpods Max because of the noise cancellation and passthrough features that they have, not to mention, they integrate seamlessly with all of my devices without any extra steps. The sound quality on them is amazing and I would definitely recommend them to anyone willing to pay the hefty price tag.
